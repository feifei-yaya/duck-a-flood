High Risk – Immediate Action Required

  • Move to higher ground immediately.
  • Avoid all floodwaters — do not drive through water.
  • Follow evacuation orders from authorities.
  • Keep emergency kit and phone with you.

Moderate Risk – Stay Alert

  • Monitor local weather updates frequently.
  • Prepare an emergency bag.
  • Charge devices and review evacuation routes.
  • Move valuables to higher areas.

Low Risk – Be Prepared

  • Stay aware of changing weather conditions.
  • Review emergency plans with household members.
  • Check drainage systems around your property.
  • Sign up for weather alerts.

Emergency Contacts

If you are experiencing a life-threatening emergency, call 9-1-1 immediately.

For non-emergency assistance in Georgia, call 1-800-GEORGIA.
